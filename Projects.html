Deep Dark

Deep Dark is game developed for Global Game Jam in 2017. The theme that year was "waves." Our idea for the game was you are little creature searching for food
on the ocean floor. Since no light is at the ocean floor the player must rely on sonar to find food and avoid enemies hiding in the dark.

Grounded

Grounded is a game developed for CMU's Create! 24 hour event. Grounded is puzzle game. You have were grounded by your parents, as part of your punishment they have taken your console and locked away in their room. So you must go through your house trying to find a key to your parents room to retrieve your console.

CPS 382 Advance Multi Media Final Project

This was my final project for CPS 382. In the game the player has an inventory, a quest to do, and a shop keeper to interact with. The player can also fight a soldier within the castle walls. With the shop keeper the player can buy and sell items.
