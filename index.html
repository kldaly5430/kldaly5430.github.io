About

My name is Kyle Daly. I am a graduate of Central Michigan University with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, graduating in May 2019. Additionally, I have an Associate in Applied Science Degree in Computer Programming from Jackson College, Jackson Michigan.

I been interested in video games for since I was young. This passion for video games has not changed, but now instead of just playing games I want to be a part of developing them. I especially enjoy playing RPGs. I like games that allow to explore, learn about the world around you. RPGs are the best because they allow you to feel as if you are apart of the world, and make decisions that shape the world. I have been part of a Dungeons & Dragons group for 5 years. Playing the game has been so amazing I have made it a personal project to re-create our Dungeons & Dragons campaign into a video game.

While a student at Central Michigan University I have been involved with the Video Game Development and Design Club. I have been the president of the club this past year. The Club has hosted a Global Game Jam, a 24-hour hackathon, and a 24 Create! event in the past two years. We worked with students that are not all that familiar with game development and programming for a few weeks prior to each of these events. This work gave fellow students the opportunity to learn outside their field of study, the opportunity to form groups and work within this group setting, and the opportunity to work with the necessary hardware.
